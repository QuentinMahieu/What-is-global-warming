Lexical

Global warming is the unusually rapid increase in Earth’s average surface temperature over the past century.

This rapid increase is demonstrated on the left graph. We can observe the yearly average temperature anomalies (compared to a predefined average temperature) on a global scale.

You can choose a country and see the evolution for each of them on the right graph. We can observe that the impact is not the same in every country due to there location on earth (Longitude). This can be observed with the climate tool of Nasa by chosing Global Temperature.
