Project Info


Global Warming is something that we hear every day. It is surrounding us and it scares us. There is confusion between Global Warming and climate change.
I was confused and I wanted to understand what this terms exactly means. I wanted to understand where this is coming from ,What it means four us and our planet.
I wanted to visualise global warming and see if we have already gone past the point of no return.

The goal of this project is to understand Global Warming, its causes and effects. Once defined, I want to make predictions up to 2100 to visualise the Global Warming curve and when we are reaching the non-return point.

Project Sources


The main analysis is based on climate-science research done by NASA, the IPCC and the NOAA.
The data was found on their websites, kaggle and on the climate change in Australia website.
