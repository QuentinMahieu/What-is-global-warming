What is really causing Global Warming?

Among some scientifics, a lot a skeptical people tend to believe that the world increase in temperature of 1°C in 2020 compare to the pre-industrial average is explained by natural causes. The discovered natural causes are the Orbit change,the Sun's temperature and the Volcanoes activities. The human causes are Deforestation,the Ozone pollution and the Greenhouse effect. The data is collected from a research from the Nasa who created a model based on 61 climate model using climate projections to explain the global warming based on each cause. (NASA's Goddard Institute for Space Studies (GISS).
