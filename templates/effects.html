What are the effects of Global Warming?

The impact of Global Warming will increase and touch you no matter where you live. There are 5 main potential effects:

People : More diseases due to air pollution, drought, storms, etc.
Extinctions : The environnement may not be hospitable for plants and animals.(Thinks about the Polar bears that have nowhere to go without ice)
Ice melting : Polar ice reflects sunlight and deflects heat; when it melts, more of that heat stays in the atmosphere.
Ocean warming : While the oceans warm, water is expanding and causing sea levels to rise. Warmer waters are killing coral reefs and krill — essential to supporting the sea food web.
Extreme wheater : While the atmosphere warms, the climate is changing, and so is the weather. More frequent and more intense storms, flooding, droughts, heat waves, and even extreme snowfalls.
